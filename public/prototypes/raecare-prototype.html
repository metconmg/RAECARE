What RAECARE Is

Three things,
one system.

Most care platforms are directories. RAECARE is an operating system — built around the reality that trust is earned in stages, and matching is a process, not a search bar.

Trusted Support Network

A verified community of caregivers who have cleared background checks, certification requirements, and onboarding standards before they are visible to a client.

Marketplace

A matching layer connecting the right caregiver to the right client by need, availability, location, specialty, and verified capability.

Ecosystem

Scheduling, payments, documentation, communication, and continuity around the match.

Finding care should not feel like gambling. RAECARE surfaces only verified, certified caregivers matched to your situation.

  • Elderly parents needing in-home support
  • Family members with special needs
  • Post-surgical or clinical recovery care
  • Daily living assistance

RAECARE is a professional network that verifies your work and puts it in front of clients who are ready to meet.

  • Certified nursing assistants and aides
  • Home health and personal care workers
  • Special needs support specialists
  • Companion care assistants
